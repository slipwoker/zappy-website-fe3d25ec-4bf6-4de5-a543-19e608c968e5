What does your support team handle?

Our support team assists with sizing guidance, order tracking, product care instructions, and general inquiries about our apparel. We're committed to making your experience smooth and helping you find the right gear for your fitness goals.

What can I expect after contacting you?

You'll receive a response within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters like order issues, we prioritize faster replies. We'll provide clear, actionable answers and follow up until your question is fully resolved.

How do I choose the right size?

Each product page includes detailed size charts with measurements for chest, waist, and inseam. We recommend measuring your current favorite athletic wear and comparing it to our charts. When in doubt, size up for a more comfortable fit during intense workouts.

How should I care for my Gear up Pro apparel?

Machine wash cold with similar colors, tumble dry low or hang dry. Avoid fabric softeners and bleach—they can break down the technical fibers that give our apparel its performance edge. Following these steps will extend the life of your gear significantly.

How durable is your apparel?

Our products are designed for daily training and built to last. We use reinforced stitching and abrasion-resistant fabrics in high-stress areas. With proper care, you can expect our apparel to maintain performance and appearance through hundreds of washes and workouts.

How is customer feedback used?

Every piece of feedback helps us improve. We analyze reviews and support conversations to identify common concerns, refine sizing, and develop new products. Your input directly influences our design process and helps us serve the fitness community better.
