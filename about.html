Questions, answered clearly.

Everything you need to know about our commitment to accessible, performance-driven apparel.

How do you keep prices affordable?

We eliminate traditional retail markups by selling directly to you. Our streamlined supply chain and focus on essential, high-performance designs mean you get scientifically-tested gear without the premium price tag.

How is fit tested?

Every garment undergoes rigorous motion testing with athletes across multiple body types. We use 3D body scanning data and real-world performance feedback to ensure optimal fit during high-intensity movement.

What materials do you prioritize?

We select moisture-wicking, breathable fabrics that balance durability with comfort. Our materials are tested for colorfastness, stretch recovery, and abrasion resistance to ensure long-term performance.

How can I request support?

Reach out through our contact page or email us directly. We respond within 24 hours and offer hassle-free returns within 30 days if the gear doesn't meet your expectations.

How should I care for my gear?

Machine wash cold with like colors, tumble dry low. Avoid fabric softeners as they reduce moisture-wicking properties. For compression garments, air drying extends elasticity life.

What durability can I expect?

Our apparel is designed for 200+ wash cycles without significant performance degradation. Reinforced seams and high-quality stitching ensure your gear withstands intense training sessions season after season.
